Instructions

  1. The ingredients must be clicked in the exact order as written in the recipes. If you make a mistake press 'Mix' and 'Send Food' to reset.
  2. Press 'Mix' once you have finished clicking all the ingredients you need.
  3. Press 'Cook'
  4. Certain foods may require you to press 'Cook' more than once. How many times you need to press it will be written under their name if required.
  5. If you'd like, you can drag the food you created to the counter.
  6. Press 'Send Food' to start making a new food.
